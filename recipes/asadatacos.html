Carne Asada Tacos

Description

This is a staple in my diet: The Asada Taco. Succulent steak paired with simple ingredients. Less is more with this one.

Ingredients

  • steak (prepared as carne asada)
  • corn tortillas
  • cilantro
  • 1/3 cup onion, finely diced
  • limes,for garnish

Steps

  1. Grill the carne asada until preferred doneness, slice against grain, chop into small pieces
  2. Assemble steak and other ingredients in corn tortillas
  3. Enjoy!
