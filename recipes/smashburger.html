Smash Burgers

Description

What's more american than burgers and smashing stuff?

Ingredients

  • ground beef
  • buns
  • american cheese slices
  • onion

Steps

  1. Prep the raw beef into small patty balls
  2. Chop onion into slices and cook until done
  3. Drop patty on heated skillet surface, press for 20 seconds with burger press
  4. Cook for about 60 seconds and then flip
  5. Add cheese and let cook for another 60 seconds or until beef is cooked through
  6. Assmble on bun and add toppings
  7. Enjoy!
