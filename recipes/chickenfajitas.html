Chicken Fajitas

Description

You know that dish that turns heads at a mexican restaurant? Bring that sizzle and smell into your kitchen.

Ingredients

  • chicken breast
  • tortillas
  • peppers(green,red,yellow)
  • onion
  • limes,for garnish
  • sour cream

Steps

  1. Cook the chicken breasts, chop into small slices
  2. Chop and cook the pepper and onions
  3. Assemble in tortilla and add desired toppings
  4. Enjoy!
